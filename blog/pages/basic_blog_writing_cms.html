Basic Blog Writing CMS

Date Published (06/22/2021 12:13 PM)


It's kind of almost an exaggeration to call this a CMS,

but technically it does manage content.


It makes writing blogs a lot easier than before since it

generates my files, and stuff. However it's extraordinarily

limited.


Thankfully my blogs are only text files, so I do not really

need much else.


If I need more, I can just use markdown, and maybe defer steps when

publishing I suppose.


Right now, it's a write-once -> commit/publish. It would be more flexible

to separate this into multiple steps, however I don't need it so I'll keep it

the way it is at the moment since it's very simple.


Yay to the power of bash.
